Welcome To Noise Controller!

Noise Controller allows you to take a group of available explorers and have them travel through different environments. BUT only if they are careful and quiet enough to not scare the animals away. Score points for each animal that appears according to how rare a sighting it is!

Let's Go!

Gather your teams and see which groups can collect the rarest of all creatures in land, air and sea! There might even be surprises inside for certain scores reach per session!
